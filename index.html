The overlay One hotkey away Press the hotkey and Overnotes fades in over your game. No alt-tabbing, no second screen required.
Sticky notes Write it down before you forget Drop a note anywhere on the infinite canvas. Colors, font sizes, rotation — it’s your board.
Freehand drawing Circle the suspicious stuff Sketch right over the game with the pen tool. Six colors, any thickness.
Region screenshots Clip evidence straight from the game Hit the screenshot hotkey, drag over a region, and the crop lands on your canvas as an image.
Subgraphs Folders, but spatial Group notes and clues into nested canvases. Double-click to dive in, breadcrumbs to climb back out.

And the fine print is nice too

No game hooks

Overnotes is a separate composited window — the same technique as Discord’s overlay. Nothing is ever injected into your game.

Per-game libraries

Documents are organized per game and autosaved as plain JSON on your disk. Your notes are always yours.

Custom hotkeys

Ctrl+Shift+E and Ctrl+Shift+S out of the box — rebind both to whatever fits your muscle memory.

Second-monitor mode

Prefer a normal window? Open any document standalone, or detach the overlay mid-session and keep editing.

Windowed & borderless

The overlay glues itself to your game’s window and follows it around — windowed and borderless-fullscreen games both work.

Free & open source

Vibe-coded with love, zero cost, all on GitHub. Found a bug? There will be some — issues and PRs welcome.
